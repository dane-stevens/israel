# Who are the Jews?

The term **Jew** is first used in 2 Kings 25:25. At this stage in the timeline, the House of Israel has been exiled into Assyria so the term **Jew/Jews** can only refer to the [House of Judah](/terms/who-is-the-house-of-judah).
